Questions owners ask us

Do I have to change my phone number?

No. You keep your number. We set up simple call forwarding so unanswered calls roll to your AI assistant. Answer the phone like normal — the AI only steps in when you can't.

What if the AI doesn't know the answer?

It never guesses. If a customer asks something outside your business info, it tells them the owner will follow up and instantly texts you their question and phone number.

Can it really quote my prices?

Yes — it quotes the price ranges you give us, always labeled as estimates confirmed on site. If you'd rather it never discuss pricing, we set it that way instead.

What's the setup process?

We collect your business details (services, prices, hours, service area) in one 30-minute call. We handle the phone setup and the US carrier compliance registration (A2P 10DLC). Most businesses are live within a week.

Is texting customers even legal?

Yes — we register your business through the official US carrier compliance program (A2P 10DLC) as part of setup, so your texts are delivered reliably and legally. The customer texted or called you first, which is the cleanest consent there is.

What if I want to cancel?

Cancel anytime with a message — no contracts, no cancellation fees. Your number and customers stay yours.
