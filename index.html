FAQ

Straight answers.

Do I keep my phone number?
Yes. CallCatch AI works behind your existing business number — the one on your truck, your cards, and Google. Nothing changes for your customers, and you don't port or forward anything.
What happens if the AI doesn't know the answer?
It says so honestly and forwards the conversation to your phone immediately. It never guesses and never makes things up — it only answers from the business info you gave it.
Can it quote my actual prices?
Yes. During setup you give us your services, price ranges, hours, and service area. The AI quotes exactly what you told it — like "$1,200–$2,500 installed depending on the unit" — and nothing else.
What does setup look like?
One 30-minute call. We collect your services, prices, hours, and service area, connect your number, and test it with you. Most businesses are live within a week — and if you're not live in 14 days, your setup fee is refunded.
Is texting my customers even legal?
Yes — when it's done right. US carriers require business texting to be registered (it's called A2P 10DLC). We handle that entire registration for you as part of setup, so your texts are compliant and actually get delivered.
What if I want to cancel?
Cancel anytime — there's no contract and no cancellation fee. You're month to month from day one.
